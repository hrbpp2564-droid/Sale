Small pill for status, category, or rank labels.

```jsx
<Badge tone="positive" dot>เติบโต</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning">เสี่ยง</Badge>
<Badge tone="accent" variant="solid" size="sm">#1</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral | accent | positive | negative | warning | info`. Variants: `soft` (default tint), `solid`, `outline`.
